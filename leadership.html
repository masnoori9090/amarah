06 — Leadership

The people behind
the practice.

Four principals. Over sixty combined years in luxury development, architecture, and the art of client experience.

Omar Al Habtoor

Founder & Chairman

Two decades setting the standard for premium residential development across the GCC. Omar founded Amarah in 2012 after a career at two of the region's most respected institutional developers, having grown frustrated with the industry's tendency to substitute scale for substance.

He sets Amarah's long-range vision, capital allocation strategy, and the non-negotiables that protect every project's integrity. He personally signs off on every material specification before ground is broken.

BSc Real Estate, UAE University Fellow, RICS Dubai Land Department Board

Layla Mansoori

Chief Design Officer

AA Dipl. Former senior associate at David Chipperfield Architects, London, where she led residential and cultural commissions across Europe and East Asia. Layla returned to the UAE in 2014 to define the spatial language of a different kind of Gulf developer.

Every plan, every elevation, every material palette passes through her studio before it reaches a contractor. She considers herself the last line of defence between the client's trust and a compromise.

AA Diploma, London David Chipperfield Architects RIBA Chartered

Hassan Reza

Chief Executive Officer

Fifteen years in real estate capital markets, spanning project finance, equity structuring, and institutional partnerships across the GCC, London, and Singapore. Hassan oversees delivery, sales, and the strategic partnerships that bring each project to life on schedule and within covenant.

Under his leadership, Amarah has maintained a 100% on-time delivery record across all seven completed projects — an achievement no UAE developer of equivalent calibre can match.

MBA, INSEAD CFA Charterholder ULI Member

Sara Bin Nasser

Head of Client Experience

Formerly General Manager at Aman Dubai and Abu Dhabi, Sara brought the hospitality industry's obsession with quiet precision to the real estate process. She built Amarah's concierge, onboarding, and residency programme from the ground up.

Every interaction — from initial enquiry to handover to the first year of residency — is designed, scripted, and continuously refined by her team. She is the reason Amarah owners stay, and refer.

Aman Hotels & Resorts, Abu Dhabi BSc Hospitality, EHL Lausanne
