01 — Our Story

We do not construct
buildings. We compose
living environments.

Twelve years, 480 residences, and an unwavering refusal to compromise.

The Practice

Restraint as a design philosophy, not a constraint.

Amarah was founded in 2012 by Omar Al Habtoor with a single conviction: that the most enduring homes are built with the same discipline an architect brings to a museum, not the urgency a developer brings to a sales target.

In a region where scale is often confused with quality, we chose a different path. Smaller developments. Longer gestation. Tighter teams. We deliberately limit our pipeline to projects we can supervise with the same granularity we bring to a single apartment's joinery.

The result is a portfolio of twelve developments — none exceeding 120 residences — each of which has held its value and reputation in ways that volume developers cannot match.

02 — Philosophy

Four principles.
Every decision.

I

Considered Materiality

Natural stone, honed travertine, smoked oak, brushed bronze. Every surface is selected for how it ages, not just how it looks on day one. We source from quarries we visit and mills we know by name.

II

Disciplined Geometry

Plans resolved on a precise grid. Volumes proportioned to the human body. Light treated as a primary material, not an afterthought. Every corridor, every threshold, every reveal is considered.

III

Lived Calm

Acoustically quieted interiors, generous thresholds, air that moves correctly. Spaces engineered for the nervous system as much as for the eye. We model acoustics before we finalise plans.

IV

Lasting Value

Built to stand beautifully over decades. We select trusted global partners and commission details twice before they meet a resident. Our buildings appreciate because they deserve to.
