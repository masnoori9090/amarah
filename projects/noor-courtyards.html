The Concept

The courtyard as
the heart of the home.

Every Noor Courtyards townhouse is organised around a private courtyard — an outdoor room, open to the sky, sheltered on three sides, planted with a curated landscape designed by Cracknell Dubai.

The courtyard is not an amenity or an upgrade. It is the plan. Every ground-floor room opens onto it. Bedrooms above look down into it. The swimming pool occupies one end; a majlis pavilion the other. Life at Noor Courtyards is lived between inside and out.

MBR City's master plan gives each cluster of homes shared access to 60 kilometres of cycling paths, Crystal Lagoon water frontage, and the largest man-made park in Dubai.
