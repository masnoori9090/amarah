The Concept

A home surrounded by nature, built to outlast fashion.

Maison Liwa is a collection of twenty-four private villas set within Al Barari — Dubai's most thoughtfully landscaped residential enclave. Each residence is positioned to maximise garden depth and botanical privacy, with no two villas sharing a sight line.

The architecture draws from the tradition of the Arabic courtyard home, reinterpreted through a contemporary lens. Double-height entrance halls open to generous living volumes. Oman limestone floors continue uninterrupted from inside to the pool terrace. Roof overhangs are calculated to shade interiors in summer while admitting winter light at the correct angle.

Materials were sourced from eleven countries. Every finish has been approved by our Chief Design Officer and cross-referenced with climate durability data for Dubai's coastal humidity.
