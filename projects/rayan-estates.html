The Concept

The rarest address in the UAE. Eighteen homes on the water's edge.

Rayan Estates is Amarah's most ambitious and most private development. Eighteen villas occupy a 600-metre stretch of Al Marjan Island's outer shore — one of the last undeveloped island waterfront parcels in the entire UAE.

The architecture was developed over three years with a structural focus on openness and lightness. Floor-to-ceiling glazing on all sea-facing elevations. Roof terraces with unobstructed horizon views. A private marina with eight berths, shared across the eighteen residences.

Rayan Estates will not be listed publicly. All enquiries are by introduction. Priority registrants will be the first — and in most cases the only — people who are offered the opportunity to purchase.

Location

Al Marjan Island — the UAE's next defining address.

Al Marjan Island is Ras Al Khaimah's flagship development: four natural coral islands extending 4.5 kilometres into the Arabian Gulf. The Wynn Al Marjan Island resort opens 800 metres from Rayan Estates in 2027. RAK International Airport is 45 minutes from Dubai; a direct helipad is 12 minutes from the island.
