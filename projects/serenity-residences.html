01 — Overview

A community
composed with care.

Serenity Residences is Amarah's most considered development to date — a gated waterfront community that brings together duplex apartments, sky-level penthouses, and a full-service private clubhouse under one architectural vision. Every block was positioned to frame the water, preserve the canopy, and protect the quiet that gives this address its name.

Inside, interiors range from the warmly resolved duplex homes — finished in honed marble, smoked oak, and hand-applied plaster — to the penthouse residences above, where crystal chandeliers, arched stone mirrors, and floor-to-ceiling glass command undivided attention.

03 — Duplex Residences

Two floors.
One story.

The duplex residences — spanning 3,200 to 5,800 sqft across two levels — are finished to a standard that does not announce itself. Honed marble on every floor, smoked-oak joinery, and plaster walls that gain warmth over time.

04 — Sky Penthouses

Above the
ordinary.

The penthouse collection — four residences occupying the top two floors — is a different category entirely. Crystal chandeliers, arched Calacatta mirrors, and private sky terraces with unobstructed waterfront views. Sizes from 6,400 to 8,400 sqft. From AED 18M.

05 — The Clubhouse

A private world
at ground level.

The Serenity Clubhouse operates as a residents-only private members venue — concierge reception, wellness facilities, a squash court, and curated communal spaces. Exclusively for owners and their guests.
