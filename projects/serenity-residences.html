01 — Overview

A community
composed with care.

Serenity is Amarah's flagship development — a curated collection of 48 quiet-luxury residences in Jakkur, north Bengaluru's most sought-after residential address. Designed for families who prefer depth over spectacle.

The duplex homes are composed around a double-height living volume, natural stone, and soft oak joinery. The signature penthouse occupies the top floor with panoramic terraces and a bespoke material palette. Garden residences open directly onto private terraces and landscaped courtyards.

The duplex residences are finished to a standard that does not announce itself — honed marble on every floor, smoked-oak joinery, and plaster walls that gain warmth over time. Two-level homes composed around a double-height living volume.
