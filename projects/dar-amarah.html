The Concept

On the shore of Abu Dhabi's most singular cultural district.

Dar Amarah occupies one of the last beachfront parcels on Saadiyat Island — 400 metres from the Louvre Abu Dhabi and walking distance to the forthcoming Guggenheim. The setting is, simply, irreplaceable.

The architecture responds to the Saadiyat shoreline through a series of barrel-vaulted colonnades in Omani limestone that shade the ground-floor living spaces and frame private beach access corridors. Each estate has its own direct access to a 40-metre stretch of white sand beach.

Phase I (24 estates) sold out within six months of launch at an average premium of 18% above initial price. Phase II releases 14 estates at revised pricing, with handover aligned to Q1 2027.
