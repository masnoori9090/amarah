01 — About

A standard we
set for ourselves.

The Serenity Clubhouse was designed alongside the residences — not as an amenity checklist, but as a natural extension of how we believe life at this address should feel.

Its marble corridors, glazed atrium, and resident reception are staffed every hour. Every facility is reserved for owners and their invited guests. Nothing here has a queue. Nothing here is shared with strangers.

This is quiet luxury made functional — a space where the standard of care you experience in your home continues the moment you step outside your door.

02 — Facilities

What awaits
inside.

Concierge & Reception

A staffed residents' lobby — your first impression every time you arrive home. 24-hour concierge, parcel handling, visitor registration, and valet service.

Private Arrival Sequence

The move from car to residence is considered. Marble corridors, private elevator lobbies, and a passage that acknowledges your arrival as the moment it is.

Squash Courts

Full-specification squash courts with professional court surfaces, spectator seating, and equipment lockers. Bookable exclusively by residents.

Grand Atrium

A glazed, double-height atrium for resident events, private functions, and quiet afternoons alike. The architectural heart of the Clubhouse.
